Peanut Butter Blossoms

Description:

One of my favorite cookies from my childhood, a tradition cookies for the holidays my mom makes. Peanut Butter cookies with Hershey's Kisses on top.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar, divided
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (9 ounce) bags milk chocolate candy kisses, unwrapped

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease or line cookie sheets.
  2. Beat shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar, and 1 cup white sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in milk and vanilla.
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into peanut butter mixture until well blended.
  5. Shape spoonfuls of dough into balls and roll in remaining 1/2 cup white sugar. Place cookies 2 inches apart on the prepared cookie sheets.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from the oven and immediately press a chocolate kiss into each cookie.
  7. Allow to cool completely; the kiss will harden as it cools.
